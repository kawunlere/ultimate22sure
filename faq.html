Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ultimate22sure.

Is Ultimate22sure free to use?
Yes, 100% free. There are no hidden charges or subscriptions. Anyone can complete tasks and unlock premium content without paying.
How do I unlock a premium link?
Simply enter your Gmail to unlock the task system, then complete every task on the list. Once all tasks are verified, your premium link will appear instantly.
Why do I need to submit my Gmail?
Submitting your Gmail helps us verify you are a real human, prevent abuse, and track your task progress so you don't lose it if the page refreshes.
Can I skip the task timer?
No. Each task requires a verification countdown to ensure the action was performed correctly. This prevents bots and fake completions.
How do I become a verified creator?
Send a request to our email kawunlere@gmail.com with your Gmail and a short description of your channel or content. We approve creators manually.
Can I run my own campaigns?
Yes! Verified creators get access to their own dashboard where they can build unlimited custom task campaigns and share unique links with their audience.
What types of tasks can I add to a campaign?
YouTube subscribe, like, comment, WhatsApp join, Telegram join, Instagram follow, TikTok follow, Facebook like, Twitter follow, and custom links.
Is my Gmail safe?
Yes. Your Gmail is used only for verification and access control. We do not sell, share, or send spam to any user.
What if a task doesn't get verified?
Make sure you complete the action fully, wait for the timer, and don't close the tab. If it still fails, contact us at kawunlere@gmail.com.
Does Ultimate22sure work on mobile?
Yes. The website is fully mobile responsive and works smoothly on all phones, tablets, and computers.
